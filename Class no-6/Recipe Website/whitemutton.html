White-Mutton-Pulao







Discriptions

White Mutton Pulao, also known as Safed Mutton Pulao, is a delicate and fragrant rice dish that hails from the Indian subcontinent. Unlike traditional pulaos, this version is characterized by its light and subtle flavors, often achieved without the use of heavy spices or colorings. Tender pieces of mutton are simmered in a blend of aromatic spices and yogurt, which not only tenderizes the meat but also imparts a creamy texture to the dish. The basmati rice is then cooked to perfection in this flavorful broth, resulting in a delectable meal that is both comforting and satisfying. White Mutton Pulao is typically garnished with fried onions, fresh coriander, and a squeeze of lemon, making it a perfect dish for festive occasions or a special family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 250 gm white mutton, cut into 1.5 cm cubes
  • 1 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chopped ginger
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh coriander
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cumin
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh turmeric
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh coriander leaves
  • 1 cup chopped fresh bell peppers (optional)
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh thyme
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh rosemary leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley leaves
